# Research Note: Purpose or Profit? How Unilever's Board Lost Control of the ESG Narrative — and What It Cost Stakeholders

## Suggested Research Direction
**Corporate Governance and ESG Oversight**

## Topic Summary
This topic examines the tension between corporate purpose and profit maximization, focusing on how board oversight influences ESG strategy, public narrative, and stakeholder outcomes.

## Key Issues
1. The role of the board in supervising ESG communication
2. Conflicts between purpose-driven branding and shareholder expectations
3. Governance failures in strategic messaging
4. Stakeholder reactions to inconsistent ESG positioning
5. The impact of ESG narrative misalignment on corporate credibility

## Possible Research Questions
- How does board oversight affect the consistency of ESG strategy and communication?
- What governance mechanisms can reduce ESG narrative drift?
- How do stakeholders respond when ESG positioning appears disconnected from business performance?

## Keywords
ESG, corporate governance, board oversight, stakeholder value, sustainability strategy, corporate purpose
